Who We Are

We at Sapphire Youngsters TV are a seasoned host of community driven by the passion of achieving academic and social excellence in our leaders of tommorow. 

Our team is made up of passionate, gifted and talented individuals; all knowledgable in their different fields of contribution and expertise; put together by the CEO and Chairman Mr. Alex Dada Linus, we have a team dedicated to pursuing and showcasing the excellence and talents of the
African child.

The Future is Here

Our Mission

At Sapphire Youngsters group, we create a world-class spotlight for teens and young adults tp boost their self-esteem and abilities. Our legendary passion is to spur their confidence in reaching full potentials in life.

Our TV reality show spanning 7 to 8 weeks showcases unscripted life experience starring teens between the ages of 12 to 18.

We are partnerning with television-giants Multichoice and other television channels across Nigeria,

A message from the CEO

Children are heritage and precious gemstones from God; that is why we use the word Sapphire' to express that value. Time and effort used on our children is more valuable than anything else. The society we live in often times does not allow the african child to express, discover or maximise his or her potentials; for this reason and many others, we at Sapphire Youngsters TV are passionate and dedicated to changing this norm.

At Sapphire Youngsters, we only stand to bring out the best of each and every child.

Our main goals are to teach and empower teenagers to discover themselves and their potentials. We have come together as a team of individuals to drive this goals for achievement.
