Who We Are

We at Sapphire Youngsters TV are a seasoned host of community driven by the passion of achieving academic and social excellence in our leaders of tommorow. 

Our team is made up of passionate, gifted and talented individuals; all knowledgable in their different fields of contribution and expertise; put together by the CEO and Chairman Mr. Alex Dada Linus, we have a team dedicated to pursuing and showcasing the excellence and talents of the African child.
